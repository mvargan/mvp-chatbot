==================================================
Q: What are examples of Public Art & Activism in Poland?
==================================================

### Short
Eco-friendly murals and pollution-fighting public art.

### Full
Examples of eco-conscious public art in Poland include:

• Eco-friendly street art using non-toxic paints
• Łódź’s internationally recognized mural scene
• A pollution-reducing mural in Warsaw
• The Converse City Forests project
• Artists Dawid Ryski and Maciek Polak
• Execution by Good Looking Studio

A mural in Warsaw uses special pollution-reducing paint to help improve air quality. It was created as part of the Converse City Forests initiative.

Q: What do artists use to make sustainable art?
==================================================

### Short
Eco-friendly and recycled materials.

### Full
Sustainable artists use:

• Non-toxic ceramic glazes
• Sustainable clay
• Energy-efficient kilns
• Recycled metals
• Fair-trade gemstones
• Organic resins
• Wood and plant-based materials

These materials reduce environmental impact and promote ethical production.

SUSTAINABLE FASHION IN POLAND
==================================================

==================================================
Q: What fabrics and practices are used by Polish designers?
==================================================

### Short
Organic, recycled and ethically produced materials.

### Full
Polish designers promote sustainability by using:

• Organic fabrics
• Recycled textiles
• Ethical and transparent production
• Eco-friendly dyeing techniques
• Local production to reduce transport emissions
• Minimal waste design (zero-waste techniques)
• Durable, timeless fashion

This approach challenges fast fashion and supports long-term sustainability.

GREEN ENERGY & ART

==================================================
Q: What impact does green energy have on art?
==================================================

### Short
It powers sustainable art installations.

### Full
Some artists use renewable energy sources, such as solar power, to operate public art installations.

Example:
• Aleksandra Stratimirovic integrates solar-powered elements into her light-based artworks.

These projects highlight the connection between renewable energy and contemporary art.
