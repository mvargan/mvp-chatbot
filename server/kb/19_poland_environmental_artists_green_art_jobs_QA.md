III. GREEN JOBS RELATED TO ART
==================================================

==================================================
Q: What green jobs are related to art?
==================================================

### Short
Environmental art and sustainable design professions.

### Full
Examples include:

1. Environmental Artist
Creates eco-focused installations, sculptures and performances.

2. Eco-Designer
Designs sustainable furniture, fashion or packaging.

3. Sustainable Architect / Landscape Designer
Designs eco-friendly buildings and public spaces.

4. Environmental Illustrator
Creates visuals for environmental campaigns.

5. Art Educator (Environmental Focus)
Teaches sustainability through art.

6. Public Art Project Coordinator (Green Projects)
Manages eco-conscious community art projects.

7. Green Set Designer
Designs sustainable sets for film and theatre.

8. Eco-Fashion Designer
Creates clothing from organic or recycled materials.

9. Sustainable Graphic Designer
Works with NGOs and green brands using ethical design.

10. Community Arts Organizer (Eco-Initiatives)
Organizes eco-art workshops and festivals.
