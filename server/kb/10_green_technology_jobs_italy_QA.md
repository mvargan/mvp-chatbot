Q: What types of green jobs are related to technology?
==================================================

### Short
Renewable energy, smart grid and sustainable IT jobs.

### Full
Green technology jobs include:
- Renewable energy technicians (solar and wind systems)
- Energy efficiency engineers
- Sustainable IT specialists
- Environmental data analysts
- Smart grid technicians
- Electric vehicle technicians

These roles show how technology supports environmental sustainability and reduces ecological impact.

==================================================
Q: How does technology contribute to sustainability?
==================================================

### Short
It reduces emissions and improves efficiency.

### Full
Technology supports sustainability by:
- Producing renewable energy (solar, wind)
- Using smart grids and energy-efficient systems
- Improving agriculture through precision farming
- Advancing recycling and waste management
- Promoting electric mobility
- Developing smart cities with IoT and data analytics

Overall, technology reduces waste, improves efficiency and lowers environmental impact.

Q: What is the future outlook for green jobs in technology?
==================================================

### Short
Very promising and growing.

### Full
The future of green tech jobs is strong due to growth in:
- Renewable energy
- Electric mobility
- Sustainable agriculture
- Circular economy industries

Engineering, IT and materials management sectors will require more green-skilled professionals. Green jobs help fight climate change while supporting economic growth and innovation.
