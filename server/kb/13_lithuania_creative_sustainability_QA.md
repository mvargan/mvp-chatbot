HERITAGE & RESTORATION
==================================================

==================================================
Q: What do restorers preserve?
==================================================

### Short
They preserve cultural, historical and artistic heritage.

### Full
Restorers preserve valuable cultural objects and structures, including:
- Fine art paintings, drawings and prints
- Historical books and manuscripts
- Photographs and film
- Sculptures and decorative objects
- Furniture and wooden artifacts
- Textiles and tapestries
- Archaeological artifacts
- Historic buildings and architecture
- Mosaics and frescoes
- Musical instruments
- Metal, ceramic and glass objects

Their goal is to protect authenticity and integrity while preventing further deterioration. Restoration requires knowledge of materials, chemistry, historical techniques and conservation ethics.

RECYCLING & CREATIVE REUSE
==================================================

==================================================
Q: What materials do recycled crafts use?
==================================================

### Short
Repurposed everyday materials.

### Full
Recycled crafts use materials such as:
- Paper (newspapers, magazines, cardboard)
- Plastic bottles and containers
- Glass jars
- Metal cans
- Fabric scraps and old clothing
- Wood pallets
- Electronic waste
- Cork, rubber, leather scraps
- Old CDs and DVDs
- Yarn from old sweaters
- Egg cartons and packaging

These materials are transformed into decor, jewelry, toys, furniture and practical objects. The goal is to reduce landfill waste and promote circular economy principles.

SUSTAINABLE DESIGN PROFESSIONS
==================================================

==================================================
Q: What does a landscape architect do?
==================================================

### Short
Designs sustainable outdoor spaces.

### Full
Landscape architects design functional and environmentally responsible outdoor environments such as:
- Parks and gardens
- Public spaces and campuses
- Stormwater systems (rain gardens, bioswales)
- Habitat restoration projects
- Urban green spaces
- Recreational and therapeutic areas

They integrate sustainability principles such as native plants, water conservation and ecosystem protection. They use CAD and GIS tools to design and visualize projects.

DIGITAL ART & SUSTAINABILITY
==================================================

==================================================
Q: How does digital art reduce waste?
==================================================

### Short
It eliminates physical materials.

### Full
Digital art reduces waste by removing the need for:
- Paper
- Canvas
- Paint
- Printing materials

It allows creative production without material consumption, reducing environmental impact.

SUSTAINABLE FASHION
==================================================

==================================================
Q: What does an eco-fashion designer do?
==================================================

### Short
Designs environmentally responsible clothing.

### Full
Eco-fashion designers:
- Use sustainable materials (organic cotton, hemp, recycled textiles)
- Apply zero-waste pattern techniques
- Use natural or low-impact dyes
- Ensure ethical supply chains
- Promote repair, reuse and recycling
- Upcycle old garments
- Reduce carbon footprint through local production
- Educate consumers on garment longevity

Their goal is to combine fashion with environmental responsibility.
