What are green skills?
Green skills are competences which improve going through the process of green transformation, aiming at reaching climate neutrality by 2050. The need to switch into more environmentally balanced ways of production and consumption has become a necessity for developed as well as for developing countries. Regarding the green competences one must remember about the goals of balanced growth, which has been actualised in 2015 for next 15 years up to 2030, exceptionally regarding goals 9 and 12.
What does Sustainable Development Goal 9 include?
Sustainable Development Goal 9 includes the target of upgrading infrastructure and retrofitting industries to make them sustainable, with increased resource-use efficiency and greater adoption of clean and environmentally sound technologies and industrial processes by 2030.
What does Sustainable Development Goal 12 include?
Sustainable Development Goal 12 includes the target of achieving the sustainable management and efficient use of natural resources by 2030.
What are Green Jobs ?
According to the International Labor Organization green jobs are the ones that contribute to maintaining or restoring the environment, to a state before its excessive degradation.
What do Green Jobs affect?
Green jobs have a positive impact on increasing energy and raw material efficiency, limiting the emission of greenhouse gases, minimalizing waste and pollution, protecting and restoring ecosystems and supporting adaptation to the effects of climate change.
What are the examples of green jobs?
Among the new job positions created in response to the green transformation processes there are: energy auditors; ESG managers, managers and specialists in decarbonization, specialists in energy transformation;
What do energy auditors do?
They identify sources of energy waste such as inefficient lighting, insulation or appliances. They develop and recommend energy-saving strategies.

What is decarbonization?
It is the process that aims at reducing and eliminating the emissions of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gas emissions from the atmosphere.
What does Energy Transformation mean?
It happens when when one type of energy turns into another type of energy. There are many different forms of energy such as electrical, thermal, nuclear, mechanical, electromagnetic, sound, and chemical
