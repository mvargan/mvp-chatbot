==================================================

### Short
Green technology uses science to protect the environment.

### Full

Q: What are examples of green skills in technology?
==================================================

### Short
Renewable energy, green IT and sustainable design.

### Full
Examples include:

1. Renewable Energy Technology
Designing and implementing solar, wind, hydro and geothermal systems.

2. Energy Efficiency Optimization
Improving energy use in hardware, software, data centers and smart grids.

3. Sustainable Design and Engineering
Using eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient manufacturing processes.

4. Green IT and Computing
Energy-efficient computing, virtualization, cloud computing and green data centers.

5. Environmental Monitoring and Analysis
Developing technologies for monitoring air, water and climate conditions.

6. Waste Reduction and Recycling Technologies
Waste-to-energy systems, recycling processes and circular economy principles.

Knowledge of environmental laws, compliance systems and sustainability reporting.
