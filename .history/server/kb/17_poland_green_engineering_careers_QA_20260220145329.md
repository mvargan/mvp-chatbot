Q: What does a nuclear reactor operator do?
==================================================

### Short
Operates and controls nuclear reactors.

### Full
A nuclear reactor operator:
- Operates or controls nuclear reactors
- Moves control rods
- Starts and stops equipment
- Monitors and adjusts control systems
- Records operational data in logs
- Ensures safe and stable reactor performance

Q: What does a Wind Turbine Technician do?
==================================================

### Short
Installs and maintains wind turbines.

### Full
A Wind Turbine Technician:
- Assembles and installs wind turbines
- Prepares construction sites and tools
- Ensures safety at the construction site
- Performs maintenance and troubleshooting
- Works at heights and in outdoor conditions
