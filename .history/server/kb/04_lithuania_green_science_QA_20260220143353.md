Q: What does a biological systems engineer do?
==================================================

### Short
Develops sustainable bioenergy and environmental solutions.

### Full
Biological systems engineers conduct experiments to develop renewable biofuels, bioenergy and sustainable biomaterials. They work on clean water systems, renewable energy and technical environmental solutions.

Q: What does a biotechnologist do?
==================================================

### Short
Uses plants and organisms to improve agriculture.

### Full
Biotechnologists apply plants and photosynthetic organisms in experiments. They aim to improve crops and agricultural sustainability.

--------------------------------------------------

Q: What does a microbiologist do?
==================================================

### Short
Studies microorganisms to solve environmental and health problems.

### Full
Microbiologists monitor and identify microorganisms in different environments. They help solve issues related to climate, food, health and agriculture. They also develop enzymes, vitamins and grow microbial cultures.

--------------------------------------------------

==================================================
Q: What does a zoologist do? (Lithuania context)
==================================================

### Short
Studies animals and protects species.

### Full
Zoologists study animal behaviour, physiology, genetics and ecology. They collect and analyze data, monitor populations and develop strategies to protect endangered species and habitats.

--------------------------------------------------

==================================================
Q: What does a marine biologist do? (Lithuania context)
==================================================

### Short
Studies marine ecosystems and pollution effects.

### Full
Marine biologists study sea animals and plants. They analyze food chains, predator-prey relationships and investigate pollution, pesticide runoff, oil spills and coastal damage.

==================================================

### Short
Studies birds and their migration.

### Full
Ornithologists study birds in laboratories and in natural habitats. They monitor migration, help build nests, provide food and treat injured birds.
