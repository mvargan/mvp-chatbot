# STEAM FOR SUSTAINABILITY – KNOWLEDGE BASE

---

## TOPIC: About the Project

### Short
- Erasmus+ project about sustainability and STEAM.
- 5 partner schools from Croatia, Italy, Greece, Lithuania and Poland.
- Focus on green skills, green jobs and environmental awareness.
- 36-month project with meetings, mobilities and workshops.

### Long
STEAM for Sustainability is an Erasmus+ project that increases awareness about environmental sustainability among young people. The project promotes green technologies and green jobs through STEAM subjects (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Mathematics). One of the project results is an English educational chatbot.

### Project Goals
- Increase awareness about environmental sustainability.
- Encourage students, especially girls, to explore green jobs.
- Develop innovative educational materials.
- Promote STEAM learning.

## Compare Countries (Short)

All partner schools work on sustainability through education.
Croatia coordinates the project.
Italy and Poland have stronger links to industry and engineering.
Lithuania emphasizes science and environmental research.
Greece is smaller in size and focuses on general education.
