# STEAM FOR SUSTAINABILITY – CORE PROJECT BASE (Q&A)

==================================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
==================================================

## Q: What is the STEAM for Sustainability project?
### Short
It is an Erasmus project that promotes green skills and green jobs through STEAM education.

### Full
STEAM for Sustainability is a 36-month Erasmus project. Its goal is to increase awareness of environmental sustainability among students. The project promotes green technologies and green jobs through STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics). It includes chatbots, workshops, mobilities and international cooperation.

### Mini Task
Name one reason why sustainability is important for young people.

--------------------------------------------------

## Q: What is the main goal of the project?
### Short
To increase awareness about environmental sustainability.

### Full
The project aims to help students understand the importance of environmental protection and green careers. It encourages especially girls to explore green jobs in STEAM fields.

--------------------------------------------------

## Q: What does STEAM mean?
### Short
Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics.

### Full
STEAM combines scientific and creative disciplines to solve real-world problems. In this project, STEAM is used to teach sustainability and green innovation.

--------------------------------------------------

## Q: How long does the project last?
### Short
36 months.

### Full
The project runs for three years and includes virtual meetings, workshops, mobilities and dissemination activities.

--------------------------------------------------

## Q: What activities are included in the project?
### Short
Virtual meetings, workshops and mobilities.

### Full
The project includes:
- 7 virtual meetings
- 5 mobilities
- 22 workshops
- 1 lecture
- 2 expert presentations
- 6 visits to relevant institutions
- 2 professional excursions
At least 200 students and 50 teachers participate.

--------------------------------------------------

## Q: How many virtual meetings have been held so far?
### Short
5 virtual meetings.

--------------------------------------------------

## Q: Where have mobilities taken place?
### Short
Greece, Poland, Croatia and Lithuania.

--------------------------------------------------

COUNTRY COMPARISON

## Q: How do partner schools differ?
### Short
They differ in size, focus and educational programmes.

### Full
Croatia focuses on general grammar school education.
Italy is a technological high school.
Greece offers general upper secondary education.
Lithuania emphasizes modern and flexible education.
